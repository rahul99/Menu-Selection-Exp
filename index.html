Welcome to HCI Menu Selection Experiment

Introduction

In this experiment, you are asked to test between "Marking Menu" and "Radial" Menu
in terms of usage. You will be asked to select a series of items in the heirarchical level.
For example, you will be asked to select "Jeans" at Menu Depth=3. This means you will need
to select "Clothing" at level-1, followed by "bottoms" at level-2 followed by "jeans" at
level-3. Do not worry if it is too convoluted. You will be give few practice sessions at the
begining of the experiment.

Usage of menus:

Marking menu:

  • Press and hold the left-mouse button (in laptops, left button of touchpad) to activate Marking Menu.
  • Drag over the item to select item at the first level
  • For the second level, drag the cursor over the item but DO NOT release the cursor. You will autom atically be re-directed to the second level. Release the cursor when selection is done.
  • Press "Next when select is complete"
  • Process to complete the remaining tasks

Radial menu:

  • Press the "Right" mouse button to activate the Radial Menu
  • "Click" on the item to select it at the first level
  • For sebsequent level, (clothing -> Shoes), Radial Menu will automatically activate
  • To reset, do the right one time.
  • click "Next" to move on to the next selection.
